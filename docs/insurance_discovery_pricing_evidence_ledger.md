Notes:
- pricing_friction_evidence_entries stores one source-backed ledger entry for each observed pricing-friction event, including retrieval_method, source_url, source_provider, access_date, and a short note describing what pricing was and was not obtainable during discovery.
- Pricing-friction entries are discovery-time observations only; they do not infer hidden downstream quote results, non-public broker pricing, or underwriting outcomes that were not visible in reviewed public sources.
- pricing_transparency_friction records the specific obstacle observed for each candidate during public pricing discovery, rather than pretending every no-price case failed in the same way.
- Candidates with no directly usable public price are explicitly separated into blocked public pricing, incomplete quote output, eligibility-gated results, failed or abandoned quote retrieval, contact/account-gated pricing, broker-led public paths, and brochure-or-marketing-only public evidence.
- A candidate can carry secondary frictions when multiple barriers were visible in reviewed evidence, but primary_friction captures the best single explanation for why usable public pricing was not obtained in this run.
- pricing_friction_event_count counts the structured pricing-friction evidence entries added per primary and secondary friction, each with retrieval metadata, reviewed source URL/provider, access date, and an explicit obtainable/not-obtainable note.
