# International / Expat Health Insurance Discovery: Global Providers

Scope: discovery-only shortlist of major international / expat health insurance providers and plan entry points relevant to a German citizen family of four living in Georgia. This file does not rank providers or build a recommendation dashboard.

Family context used for relevance screening:
- German citizen family of four
- Resident in Georgia
- Adults age 42 and 37
- Two children
- Preferred cover geography: Georgia and Germany, ideally worldwide excluding the US

## Global provider section

1. Cigna Global
- Official source: https://www.cignaglobal.com/
- Quote path: https://www.cignaglobal.com/quote/pages/quote/CountrySelector.html
- Evidence: Cigna states it offers international health insurance for individuals and families with access to medical support in over 200 markets and territories.
- Discovery note: strong global expat/family candidate; Georgia/Germany and worldwide-ex-US configuration still need quote or policy verification.

2. Allianz Care
- Official source: https://www.allianzcare.com/en/personal-international-health-insurance.html
- Quote path: https://my.allianzcare.com/myquote/5
- Evidence: Allianz says its Care international health insurance plans are designed for people who spend long periods overseas and includes families, professionals, students, and nomads.
- Discovery note: strong expat-family candidate; geography and pricing still need deeper verification.

3. Bupa Global
- Official source: https://www.bupaglobal.com/en/private-health-insurance
- Quote path: same page / selector-driven
- Evidence: the official individuals-and-families selector includes both Georgia and Germany in the country list.
- Discovery note: one of the strongest public leads for this family context because both target countries are visibly present on the official plan selector.

4. AXA Global Healthcare
- Official source: https://www.axaglobalhealthcare.com/en/international-health-insurance/
- Quote path: https://www.axaglobalhealthcare.com/en/international-health-insurance/get-a-quote/?camp=online-default
- Evidence: AXA positions the product for people living abroad or travelling overseas frequently and says treatment is available within the selected region of cover.
- Discovery note: promising for worldwide / regional cover structures; exact Georgia/Germany fit still needs confirmation.

5. APRIL International
- Official source: https://www.april-international.com/en/long-term-international-health-insurance
- Entry path: https://www.april-international.com/en
- Evidence: APRIL positions the product for expatriates and globally mobile individuals staying abroad longer than 12 months and references worldwide cover depending on the selected area.
- Discovery note: strong long-term-expat candidate; Germany was visible in extracted selector examples, Georgia still unverified in this run.

6. William Russell
- Official source: https://www.william-russell.com/international-health-insurance/
- Evidence: William Russell explicitly markets international health insurance for expats and people living, working, or studying abroad.
- Discovery note: relevant private IPMI candidate; geography specifics remain to be checked.

7. MSH International
- Official source: https://www.msh-intl.com/en/individuals/
- Quote path: https://www.msh-intl.com/en/comparisons-offers-msh
- Evidence: MSH presents First'Expat+ as a comprehensive, flexible international health insurance plan and positions itself as a healthcare partner for mobility needs.
- Discovery note: relevant expat insurer; needs deeper document review for Georgia/Germany coverage specifics.

8. Now Health International
- Official source: https://www.now-health.com/en/insurance-plans/
- Evidence: Now Health publicly compares SimpleCare and WorldCare international plans and shows geography-sensitive benefit structures including separate USA treatment handling.
- Discovery note: useful IPMI candidate for later pricing/document review.

9. IMG (International Medical Group)
- Official source: https://www.imglobal.com/
- Entry path: https://www.imglobal.com/international-health-insurance
- Evidence: IMG states its international health insurance provides annually renewable private medical coverage for expats and global citizens living or working internationally.
- Discovery note: relevant but needs care to distinguish full expat medical products from travel-medical products.

10. Aetna International
- Official source: https://www.aetnainternational.com/
- Related source: https://www.aetna.com/employers-organizations/aetna-international-insurance.html
- Evidence: Aetna International says it supports globally mobile members and offers provider network access in 200+ countries and territories.
- Discovery note: clearly a major global provider, but retail individual/family availability is less explicit on the landing page than with some competitors.

11. Foyer Global Health (backup candidate)
- Official source: https://www.foyerglobalhealth.com/
- Comparison page: https://www.foyerglobalhealth.com/plans-services/our-plans/international-health-insurance-comparison/
- Evidence: official search result describes Foyer as an international health insurance provider for expats, digital nomads, and globally mobile people.
- Discovery note: included as a backup so the shortlist stays above the required minimum if one of the top 10 later drops out.

## Explicit uncertainties
- Many providers gate exact pricing, underwriting, area-of-cover options, and eligibility behind quote forms.
- For several candidates, Georgia residence acceptance and a true worldwide-excluding-US option were not directly verifiable from the public landing pages reviewed in this run.
- This run intentionally stops at discovery and evidence capture, without scoring or recommendation ranking.
